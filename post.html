5 Surprising Benefits of Contributing to Open Source [Beginner Edition] 🚀

#beginners #opensource #career #github

Hey peeps 👋

Hope everyone is doing fantastic!

In today’s blog, lets look into why people really want to contribute to open source. 🤔

“For their career growth” you say?

Evidently, that’s a big component..

However, did you know that it is actually one of the few reasons why you should do it? 😮

Let’s see together the 5 reasons of why its absolutely brilliant to become an open source coder 👯‍♀️

tl;dr

  1. Build cool sh*t 👷‍♀️
  2. Be a Real Sponge → learn, learn, learn 🧠
  3. Become a true socialite: a very positive effect on network quality 📈
  4. Become the “trends” jedi master 📰
  5. Make tangible impact on the world 🌍

1. Build cool sh*t🏗️

Let’s start with a bang: if you are a creator at heart (and I know you are!) then you just get to build and ship cool stuff! 👷‍♀️

If you are interested in coding then I’m sure that growing up you enjoyed building, creating and experimenting with things.

By contributing to open source, you will continue to do exactly that whilst working on projects you are passionate about. The craziest thing is that some of these projects have the potential to change the world as we know it! 🌎

2. Be a Real Knowledge Sponge 🧽

Contributing to open source can help you demonstrate your skills to potential employers - that’s a fact.

This is the reason we built quine.sh where we help any developers (from beginner to senior) find the right projects to contribute to on Github.

However, sharing your contributions on GitHub allows you to meet other outstanding coders.

The saying “you are the average of the 5 people you hang out the most with” rings very true here.

If you spend time with people who are better than you at coding, you will just get sooo much better than if you learn by yourself. 🚀
